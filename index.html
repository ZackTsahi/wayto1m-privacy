Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 June 2026

Way to $1M (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is a personal portfolio and AI-assistant application for Android. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Summary: The app has no backend server of its own. Your portfolio and settings live on your device. Data only leaves your device when (a) you sign in (handled by Clerk), (b) you use the AI features (sent to the AI provider you choose), or (c) the app fetches public market data (only ticker symbols are sent). We do not sell your data and we run no analytics or tracking.

1. Who is responsible for your data

This app is published by an individual developer. For any privacy question or request, contact: wayto1m@outlook.com.

2. Data the app handles

Stored only on your device (not on any server we operate):

  • Your portfolio holdings, transactions, watchlist, and value history
  • Your app settings, including the AI API key you enter
  • Theme and notification preferences

This data is kept in your device’s app storage and is removed when you uninstall the app. The app has no server, so we never receive a copy of it in the background.

Sent off your device only in these specific situations:

We do not operate analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not build a profile of you and we do not sell or rent any data.

3. The AI features and your data

The AI features are optional and require an API key that you supply for the provider of your choice. When you use them, the content of your request — which may include your holdings, the questions you ask, and images you attach — is sent directly from your device to that provider so it can generate a reply. That provider processes the request under its own privacy policy and terms:

  • Anthropic
  • OpenAI
  • Google AI

We do not store or log your AI requests or responses.

4. Permissions the app may ask for

  • Camera / Photos — only when you choose to attach a screenshot or photo to the AI chat. The image is sent to your chosen AI provider for analysis.
  • Microphone — only when you use voice input; speech is transcribed for the feature you are using.
  • Notifications — to deliver the optional daily briefing you enable.

You can decline or revoke any of these in your device settings; the related feature simply won’t work without it.

5. Security

Connections to authentication, AI, and market-data services use HTTPS encryption in transit. Your authentication token is stored using the device’s encrypted secure storage. Because your portfolio data stays on your device, its security also depends on your device’s own protections (screen lock, encryption).

6. Data retention and deletion

  • On-device data: remove it any time by clearing your holdings in the app, or by uninstalling the app (which deletes all local data).
  • Account data (email/identity held by Clerk): you can request deletion of your account by contacting us at the email below; we will action it through our authentication provider.
  • We retain no other personal data, because we operate no server that stores it.

7. Children

This app is not directed to children and is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the effective date above and post the new version at the same URL.

9. Not financial advice

The app’s AI output and portfolio figures are for informational purposes only and are not financial advice. Always do your own research.
